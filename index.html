Company Overview

Galane Group (Pty) Ltd is a leading provider of integrated solutions in landscaping, cleaning materials and cleaning equipment. Established in 2020, our company is committed to delivering high-quality products and services that enhance the aesthetics, functionality, and cleanliness of residential, commercial and industrial environments.

With a strong focus on sustainability and innovation, Galane Group (Pty) Ltd strives to create healthier and more beautiful spaces for our clients. We are dedicated to providing exceptional service and products that meet the highest standards of quality, efficiency, and environmental responsibility.
